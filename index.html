Why I call it SuperfreshDEV ?

Read Text
Show More
The name SuperfreshDEV is divided into 3 words. Once Super, Fresh and DEV. The word fresh came to mind after a strenuous day of fitness after taking a cold shower. Taking a cold shower gave me a positive feeling of being happy, feeling clean and special to want to tackle the next things energetically and clearly - in short, to simply approach things "freshly".

I want to transfer this feeling to my personality and especially to my activities.
So when things are approached, they do so with energy and in the form of an abstract, structuring way of thinking.
In short, let's do things fresh.

The word super is simply an amplification of fresh and means that things will appear super fresh.

The word DEV is the abbreviation for development and primarily refers to the focus of the Software development.

If you put all these 3 words together it means someone who wants to create super fresh software solutions of the future.

That's it. Crazy, right ?
