Short Description
It's an Web-Plattform which you can work for yourself or in a team.
It comes with my smart concept of an Scrum & Kanban Dashboard to follow your Project Processes and your personality working routines.

Some Developing Aspects are inspired from the knowledge of the Book 'The 1% Method' of James Clear.
Advantages
Clean , sexy & powerful layouts which inspires you to work with it
Clean overview about your Project Processes in my Scrum Dashboard. With playful gaming features it makes fun to work with it.
Clean overview about my smart Kanban Tool where you can manage your daily working task on a next productive level as before. It helps your to reflect your results and do it next time better.
My First Version is for free. You can use it for yourself or in a Team of maximum 10 Members.
